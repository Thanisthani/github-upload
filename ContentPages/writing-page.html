Lesson 01

Message

+

1.1.What is a message

    -Message is a short communication sent from one person to another when one person cannot speak to another person directly.


1.2.Structure of Message


1.3.Example of a message

-Imagine you are a student and today you have after school class and you forgot to tell your mother. Write a message to inform your mother.

MESSAGE
30.08.2021  11.00a.m.
Dear Mother,
    I am late today because I have after school class. I will come at 5.30p.m.
Nick


1.4.Tips to write a message

  • .Ensuring language which you use to write a message can be readable and understandable for the reader.
  • Do not exceed the word limit of 50 words.
  • Do not add extra information.
  • Indirect or reported speech must be used.
  • Write simple and short sentences
Lesson 02

Essay

+

What is an essay?

    An essay is a piece of writing outlining the writer's perspective or story.


Steps to write an essay

  1. Preparation Part
    • Choose your topic
    • Do a research
    • Create an essay outline
  2. Writing Part
    • Write an introduction
    • Write body by organizing into paragraphs
  3. Revision
    • Check the content
    • Proofread for language errors

Structure of an essay

  1. Introduction

         - You should write in the introduction what your essay is about and what points it will cover.

  2. Body

         - You can plan this by identifying main points and write a paragraph on each point.

  3. Conclusion

         - This may be a summary or evaluation of contents.


Lesson 03

Notice

+

What is an notice?

Notice is formal documentation which includes the fact of observing or paying attention to something targeting at a particular person or a group of persons.


Structure of Notice


Example of Notice

Imagine you are the president of Computer Science Society of your university.Write a notice to invite members of Science Society for annual meeting

Computer Science Society of Sabaragamuwa University
NOTICE
Inviting for Annual Meeting

Computer Science Society of Sabaragamuwa University will be held annual meeting on Monday of 6th Sepetember 2021 at 2.00p.m. in Main hall.We hope to discuss about our annual exhibition and invite for all the members to participate to this.

Amashi Perea

President of Computer Science Society

Tips to write a notice

  • Sentences should be short and simple
  • Use passive voice sentences as far as possible.
  • The ideal length of notice is 50 words.
  • Include important points into notice following 5'Ws
    1. What - what is notice about?
    2. Where - where will be held?
    3. When - When is the time and the date of the event or meeting
    4. Who - who the notice is addressed to
    5. Whom - final detail should be whom to contact or get in touch with
