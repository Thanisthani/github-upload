All about Airport:

Airline

noun

an organization providing a regular public service of air transport on one or more routes.

"airline pilot"

Baggage

nonu

suitcases and bags containing personal belongings packed for travelling; luggage.

"we collected our baggage before clearing customs"

Boarding

noun

the action of getting on or into a ship, aircraft, or other vehicle.

"remember to buy your tickets before boarding"

Customs

noun

the place at a port, airport, or frontier where officials check incoming goods, travellers, or luggage.

"we were through customs with a minimum of formalities"

Passport

nonu

an official document issued by a government, certifying the holder's identity and citizenship and entitling them to travel under its protection to and from foreign countries.

"a British citizen with a valid passport does not need a visa to visit the US"

Runway

noun

a strip of hard ground along which aircraft take off and land.

"he jet braked hard as its wheels touched the runway"

Captain

noun

the pilot in command of a civil aircraft.

"aircraft captains are entitled to deny boarding to passengers under the influence of drugs"

Stewardess

noun

a woman employed to look after the passengers on a ship or aircraft.

"Anthony cheated on Lopez with an airline stewardess."

Life vest

noun

another term for life jacket.

"Kathy pulled her arms inside the life vest."
